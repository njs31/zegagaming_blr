Refund Policy

No Refunds

We regret to inform you that ZEGA GAMING does not offer refunds under any circumstances. Once a payment has been made for our services, it is considered final and non-refundable.

Why No Refunds?

At ZEGA GAMING, we strive to provide the best gaming experience possible. Our services, including gaming sessions, tournaments, and monthly passes, are designed to be used immediately upon purchase. Due to the nature of these services, we are unable to process refunds once the service has been accessed or the event has taken place.

Exceptions

In rare cases, such as technical issues on our end that prevent you from accessing our services, we may offer compensation in the form of additional gaming time or other benefits. However, this is at the sole discretion of ZEGA GAMING and does not constitute a refund.
